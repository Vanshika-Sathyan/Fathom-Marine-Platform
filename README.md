Fathom Marine Consultants — Digital Platform
A cloud-native, microservice-based digital platform supporting credential management, learning & development, employee wellbeing, and AI-driven insights.
Fathom Marine Consultants needed a unified digital platform to manage:
1.	Credential Management — Issue, verify, and track marine certifications
2.	Learning Management System (LMS) — Training programs, material uploads, progress tracking
3.	Wellbeing Module — Employee surveys, anonymous feedback, compliance tracking
4.	AI Integration — Personalized training recommendations, compliance risk detection
